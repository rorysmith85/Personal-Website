Background

I moved to Cardiff from Sweden in September 2017 to complete a MSc in Computational and Data Journalism at Cardiff University. I previously worked with refugee integration in Gothenburg, Sweden while freelancing as a journalist on the side.

As a journalist I've written on an eclectic range of issues from Afghani Refugees, to Food Justice, to the environmental effects of illegal marijuana farms in California, to organized crime in Mexico.

My current list of things to investigate and write about includes: human trafficking, the arms trade and the privatization of security, organized crime - especially investigating it from an entrepreneurial theory perspective - food justice and farming, and immigration.
